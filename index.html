The Indian Air Force (IAF) defends the Indian airspace and supports land and naval operations. It was officially established on 8 October 1932. It also provides logistics during calamities and medical emergencies. Training is given at prestigious institutions like AFA Hyderabad and NDA. Officers are trained in flying, meteorology, engineering, and more. The Air Force Day is celebrated on 8 October.
